Job Description

We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our team. Data and Analytics is a critical team within Business Technology. Our mission is to enable integrated data layers for all of Samsara and Samsara customers with the insights, tools, infrastructure, and consultation to make data-driven decisions. We are a growing team that loves all things data! The team will be composed of data engineers, architects, analysts, and data scientists. We are passionate about leveraging world-class data and analytics to deliver a great customer experience.

You should apply if:

  • You want to impact industries that power our world — driving safety, sustainability, and innovation.
  • You want to shape your own career growth in a high-performance, inclusive environment.
  • You thrive in a fast-paced, data-driven company that values creativity and teamwork.

In this role, you will:

  • Develop and maintain E2E data pipelines and automation systems using modern cloud tools.
  • Work with data from CRM, Product, Marketing, and Order Flow sources to deliver insights.
  • Ensure data quality, availability, and reliability across multiple teams.
  • Champion Samsara’s cultural values across all workstreams and collaborations.

Minimum Requirements:

  • 8+ years as a Data Engineer with experience in production-grade data pipelines.
  • Proficiency in Python, SQL, and modern ETL frameworks (DBT, Fivetran, etc.).
  • Experience with AWS, GCP, or Azure, including Databricks, Snowflake, or BigQuery.
  • Experience collaborating across teams and mentoring junior engineers.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Spark, Terraform, and serverless AWS architecture.
  • Knowledge of monitoring tools such as Splunk or DataDog.
