We’re Hiring: Senior Data Engineer

Location: US/Canada (Remote) — excluding San Francisco Bay Area and New York Metro City.

About Samsara

Samsara is the pioneer of the Connected Operations Cloud — empowering the people who keep our world moving. Our platform enables organizations across diverse industries to harness IoT data to improve efficiency, safety, and sustainability at scale.

Job Description

We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our team. Data and Analytics is a critical team within Business Technology. Our mission is to enable integrated data layers for all of Samsara and Samsara customers with the insights, tools, infrastructure, and consultation to make data-driven decisions. We are a growing team that loves all things data! The team will be composed of data engineers, architects, analysts, and data scientists. We are passionate about leveraging world-class data and analytics to deliver a great customer experience.

Our team promotes an agile, collaborative, supportive environment where diverse thinking, innovative design, and experimentation are welcomed and encouraged. This role is open to candidates residing in the US and Canada except San Francisco Bay Area and New York Metro City.

You should apply if:

  • You want to impact industries that run our world — helping keep operations efficient, sustainable, and safe.
  • You are the architect of your own career — thrive in a high-growth, fast-paced environment with endless learning opportunities.
  • You’re energized by solving real-world challenges with data and want to innovate at global scale.
  • You want to be surrounded by a team that supports, challenges, and celebrates together.

In this role, you will:

  • Develop and maintain end-to-end data pipelines, backend ingestion, and Samsara’s Data Platform for advanced analytics.
  • Work with CRM, Product, Marketing, and Support data to build reliable data products and enable automation.
  • Enhance data architecture, quality, monitoring, and observability using SQL, Python, and cloud tools.
  • Collaborate across teams to ensure interoperability of data between systems and applications.
  • Champion and embed Samsara’s cultural principles: Focus on Customer Success, Build for the Long Term, Adopt a Growth Mindset, Be Inclusive, and Win as a Team.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, or equivalent field.
  • 8+ years of experience as a Data Engineer with large-scale production pipelines.
  • Proficiency in Python, SQL, and cloud-based ETL frameworks (DBT, Fivetran, etc.).
  • Hands-on experience with AWS, GCP, or Azure — including Databricks, BigQuery, or Snowflake.
  • Strong understanding of data warehousing, transformation, and data lake architecture.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Spark, Terraform, and serverless AWS services (Lambda, S3, SQS).
  • Familiarity with observability tools such as DataDog, Splunk, or CloudWatch.
  • Experience mentoring junior engineers and leading cross-functional projects.
